Probabilistic Model¶

Each Team has a offence and defence strength (distribution) with prior (Note that the average goals per game ≈3≈3):

offencei∼(μ=1.5,τ=1)offencei∼N(μ=1.5,τ=1)
defencei∼(μ=0,τ=1)defencei∼N(μ=0,τ=1)

N is the Gaussian distribution with parameters

  • mean: μμ
  • precision: τ=1/σ2τ=1/σ2

The number of goals that team ii scores against team jj is Poission distributed with

goalsij=Poisson(λ=(offencei−defencej))goalsij=Poisson(λ=(offencei−defencej))

def print_toto_predition(k,h,i,j, result=None):
    par = mcmc.trace("params")[k:h]

    skill_away = ( mcmc.trace("offence%i"%j)[k:h] + mcmc.trace("defence%i"%j)[k:h])
